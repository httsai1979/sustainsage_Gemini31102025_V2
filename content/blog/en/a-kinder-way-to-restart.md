Momentum returns when actions fit your current capacity.

## Why kind beats tough when restarting
Pressure can freeze us. Kind structure plus tiny experiments create movement without drama.

## Three moves to try this week
- Cut the step in half—then in half again.
- Write one line you can actually send.
- Review learning, not success.
